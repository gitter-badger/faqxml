C

Corinthian column. Corinthian columns are slender and fluted. Their capitals are bell-shaped decorated with acanthus leaves. See also Doric column

Doric column. Doric columns are stout, typically 6 to 7 times as tall as the diameter at the base. See also column of the Corinithian order.

O

Orders of classical architecture. There are five classical orders: Ionic, Doric, Corinthian, Tuscan and Composite. Read more at Wikipedia.

Back to Top

V

Vitruvius. Marcus Vitruvius Pollio was an architect and engineer of the first century BCE. His book De Architectura summarises classical architecture.
